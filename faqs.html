Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a CV?

Click on the 'Create CV' button on the homepage and fill in your personal and professional details in the form provided.

Can I preview my CV before downloading?

Yes! After filling in your information, you can preview your CV before making any payment or downloading it.

Is my data saved on your servers?

No, SmartCV is designed to be privacy-focused. Your data is processed locally in your browser and is not stored unless explicitly allowed by you.

Do I have to pay to download my CV?

You can preview for free, but to download the professionally formatted PDF version, a small fee is required.
