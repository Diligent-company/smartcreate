Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how SmartCV collects, uses, and safeguards your personal data. By using this website, you agree to the terms outlined below. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency and care.

1. Information We Collect

SmartCV is designed to operate primarily within your browser. By default, we do not collect or store any personal data on our servers. Data entered for CV creation is processed in real-time and cleared upon session termination unless saved intentionally by the user.

2. Use of Information

All personal information you provide is used solely to generate your CV and preview it. We do not share, sell, or disclose your data to any third party.

3. Cookies and Tracking

SmartCV may use limited cookies to enhance your browsing experience, such as remembering preferences or session state. These cookies do not store any personally identifiable information.

4. Third-Party Services

We may integrate secure third-party tools (e.g., payment gateways) which follow their own privacy policies. Users are encouraged to review those policies before providing sensitive data.

5. Data Security

SmartCV ensures that all interactions and data processing occur in a secure environment. Your CV data is not transmitted or stored externally unless explicitly downloaded by you.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of SmartCV constitutes acceptance of the current version.

8. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Contact page.
